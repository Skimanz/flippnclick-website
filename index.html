How It Works

How Flippn'Click Works

Flippn'Click is an innovative aluminium framing system which can be used with decking and tiles. The clever design lets you change from tiles to decking without changing your framing.

Just flip the joist and insert the fasteners for tiles or a nail free timber solution.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the benefits over traditional materials?

Aluminium is lightweight, durable, and resistant to rust, rot, and insects, making it a long-lasting and low-maintenance option for decking framing.

Is it easy to install?

Absolutely! The system is designed for straightforward installation, often requiring fewer tools and less labour compared to traditional wood framing.

Can it support heavy loads?

Yes — the system is engineered to support heavy loads and is suitable for various applications, including residential and commercial projects.

Is it environmentally friendly?

Yes, aluminium is recyclable and produces less waste during production compared to some traditional materials.

Can it be used in low-profile scenarios?

The system is ideal for low-profile applications, providing a sleek, streamlined appearance while maximising headroom.

Can I use different decking materials?

The system is compatible with tiles and traditional wood, giving you flexibility in your design choices.
